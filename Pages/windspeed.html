Wind Speed

There is not a strong correlation with wind speed as one moves away from the equator (0 degrees latitude). It is interesting that the 6 highest reported wind speeds are in the far northern hemisphere (>60 degrees latitude). This could mean that it gets more windy towards the poles, but not until one passes 60 degrees latitude (Note: There aren't any cities in this dataset from >60 degrees south of the equator).
