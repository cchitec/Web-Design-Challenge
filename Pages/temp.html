Max Temperature

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 degrees latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This is due to it being early fall in the southern hemisphere and early spring in the northern half of the world.
